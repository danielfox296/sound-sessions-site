01

Descent

You lie down among twenty or so people who came for the same reason you did. For the better part of an hour there is only sound, most of it pitched low enough that you feel it in your chest before you hear it.

02

The floor

The sound takes you further down than you can take yourself. Near the end there is an invitation to sit with one other person in silence for a few minutes, and if you would rather stay on the floor, that is what the floor is for.

03

Return

The sound brings you back up, and the night is over. Most people leave lighter than they came in, and some leave without something they had been carrying for a long time.
