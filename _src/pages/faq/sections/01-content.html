What happens at a sound session

Plain answers. If yours isn't here, ask at the door.

A facilitated group experience built on engineered sound. You lie down in a dark room with one light, and for the better part of an hour there is nothing to do and nothing to say, just sound, most of it pitched low enough that you feel it in your chest before you hear it. Sessions run about 75 minutes in Denver.
You drop further than you can get on your own. Near the end you can sit with one other person in silence for a few minutes, or stay resting on the floor; both are ordinary choices in this room. Then you come back up and the night is over.
Something soft. Mats and blankets are already there. Phones stay in your bag. Doors close at start time and stay closed.
Partner studios around the Denver metro and the Front Range. Each event page lists its room and date.
